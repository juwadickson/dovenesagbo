Meet Monsieur Dovene SAGBO

Your Guide to the French Language and Culture

Introduction

Monsieur Dovene SAGBO is a passionate teacher with over 10 years of experience teaching French to English speakers. Previously, he spent 5 years teaching English to French speakers, making him a highly skilled bilingual educator. A graduate of the University of Lome, he uses innovative methods to make learning French both enjoyable and effective. His mission is to help every student achieve their language goals and develop a deep appreciation of French culture, giving them an advantage in today's competitive job market.

Teaching Philosophy

"I believe in creating a supportive and engaging environment where every student feels empowered to express themselves, take risks, and embrace the beauty of the French language and culture. My teaching approach is student-centered, encouraging hands-on activities and interactive sessions that make learning both fun and meaningful."
